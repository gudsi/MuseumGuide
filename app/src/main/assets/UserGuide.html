Art in my area

Via clicking on the "Art in my area" navigation point you can scan your environment to detect artefacts around. This works with bluetooth. If your Bluetooth and/or your GPS is switched off, you will be asked to switch on to enable the scan. A list of all artefacts in range is displayed. The artefacts are ordered ascending by distance to your phone.

Show Artefact

By clicking on one artefact of the list you are directed to the detailed view of the artefact. The location, a description as well as a small impression of the artefact are provided.

Look-up art

If you are looking for a piece of art and you know the title of it, you can look it up via the navigation point "Look-up Art". A list of all artefacts in the museum is provided. Via entering the title the artefacts are filtered. Again, by clicking on one artefact you are directed to the detailed view of the artefact. See Show Artefact

List of art in museum

The navigation point "List Of Art" provides a list of all artefacts available in the museum. By clicking on one, you are directed to the detailed view. See Show Artefact

Information

The information point provides a list of Exhibitions, Rooms and Amenities in the museum.

Exhibitions

By clicking on one of the exhibitions in the list, you are directed to the detailed view of the exhibitions. The location, the description, the opening hours as well as the exhibition period are displayed.

Rooms

By clicking on one of the rooms in the list, you are directed to the detailed view of the rooms. The location, the description and the corresponding exhibition are displayed.

Amenities

By clicking on one of the amenities in the list, you are directed to the detailed view of the amenities. The location and the description are displayed.
